About Us

Locally Owned in the Ozarks!

It's not an event without a SWEET! We bake all our cakes to order so they are never frozen. Our frostings are made from scratch, whether you choose traditional Buttercream or Italian Meringue, it's sure be scrumptious.
We personialize your sweets with your concept and our attention to detail, while making sure a lot of love is mixed into it.

We can deliver and setup your cake too!! That way, you can focus on the party!
